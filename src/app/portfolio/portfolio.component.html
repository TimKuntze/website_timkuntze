About me

Hi, I'm Tim from Berlin! After nearly 10 years doing Marketing for Amazon.de, ATOSS Software, shyftplan and others, I started to learn to develop websites and apps in summer 2020. And yes, so much can be said about Corona on the positive side: it really made one think about what you really want to do with your career! How I learned my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and AngularJS? I started a developer course with the Munich-based Developer Akademie, besides my full-time job as a Marketing Manager at shyftplan. And what can I say? It worked! Including a lot of ups and downs, help from my tutors, not being afraid to ask a lot of questions and plain commitment.
